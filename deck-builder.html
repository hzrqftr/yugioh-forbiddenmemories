Deck Builder

Track the cards you own and build decks from them. Everything saves in your browser; export a backup or sync across devices with your Google account.

collection

      suggestions

      Owned cards outside this deck, judged by ATK/DEF, fusion synergy & Guardian-Star coverage. Heuristic — no card-effect data.

        google drive sync

        Sync across devices via a private file in your own Google Drive — nothing stored publicly.

        manual backup

        Export saves your collection & decks to a JSON file; Import loads one back (or moves it to another browser).
